# 10 Easy 5-Ingredient Recipes for Quick Delicious Meals | IngreGO Blog

Cooking does not need to be complicated. Many delicious meals require only a few simple ingredients. With the right ideas, you can create impressive dishes in minutes. These five-ingredient recipes help you save time, reduce stress, and enjoy great flavors without long prep. They work for busy days, small budgets, and beginners who want easy wins in the kitchen.

## Creamy Garlic Pasta

This pasta dish offers comfort with minimal effort. Cook your pasta and save a little water. Melt butter in a pan and add garlic.

**Ingredients:**

- Pasta
- Butter
- Garlic
- Heavy cream
- Parmesan cheese

Mix in cream and parmesan until smooth. Add the pasta and stir until the sauce coats every piece. The result is rich, warm, and ready in no time. This dish takes less than 20 minutes from start to finish.

## Honey Soy Chicken

![5-ingredient honey soy chicken quick recipe](/images/blog/5-ingredient-honey-soy-chicken-recipe.svg)

Honey and soy sauce make a perfect match. Mix them with garlic and pour over chicken thighs. Bake until golden and tender.

**Simple Steps:**

1. Mix honey, soy sauce, and garlic
2. Pour over chicken thighs
3. Bake at 400°F for 25 minutes
4. Serve with rice or vegetables

The sauce becomes sticky and flavorful. Serve with rice or vegetables for a fast and satisfying dinner. This recipe works well for meal prep and can be stored for several days. ## Tomato Basil Soup

This simple soup delivers bold flavor. Cook tomatoes with onions until soft. Blend the mix until smooth.

**Soup Ingredients:**

- Tomatoes
- Onions
- Fresh basil
- Heavy cream
- Salt and pepper

Add fresh basil and a splash of cream. Heat the mixture until warm and silky. The soup tastes fresh and pairs well with bread. Perfect for a quick lunch or light dinner option.

## Avocado Egg Toast

A quick and healthy meal starts with toasted bread. Smash ripe avocado and spread it on the toast. Add a fried or poached egg on top.

**Toast Tips:**

- Use thick, crusty bread
- Choose ripe avocados
- Cook egg to your preference
- Add salt and pepper

Sprinkle salt and pepper. The result feels balanced, creamy, and perfect for breakfast or lunch. This dish provides protein and healthy fats to start your day right.

## Baked Parmesan Potatoes

Crispy potatoes require only a few steps. Slice potatoes into wedges and toss them with olive oil. Add parmesan, garlic powder, and salt.

**Potato Preparation:**

- Cut into even wedges
- Toss with oil and seasonings
- Bake at 425°F for 30 minutes
- Flip halfway through

Bake until golden and crisp. These potatoes taste rich and crunchy without extra ingredients. They make an excellent side dish or can stand alone as a snack.

## Lemon Butter Salmon

This dish offers bright flavors with almost no prep. Place salmon on a baking sheet. Add lemon slices, butter, garlic, and salt.

**Salmon Steps:**

1. Place salmon on baking sheet
2. Top with lemon slices and butter
3. Add garlic and salt
4. Bake at 375°F for 15 minutes

Bake until flaky and tender. The combination delivers a fresh and balanced taste. It works well for weeknight meals. According to research from the American Heart Association, salmon provides essential omega-3 fatty acids that support heart health.

## Chicken Quesadillas

![5-ingredient chicken quesadillas quick meal](/images/blog/5-ingredient-chicken-quesadillas-meal.svg)

Quesadillas are fast and flexible. Fill tortillas with shredded chicken and cheese. Add salsa for extra flavor.

**Quesadilla Assembly:**

- Warm tortillas
- Add shredded chicken
- Top with cheese
- Add salsa
- Cook until crisp

Cook them in a pan until the tortillas turn crisp and the cheese melts. Cut into slices for an easy and tasty meal. Perfect for using leftover chicken from previous meals. Learn [5 genius ways to reuse leftovers for tasty new meals](/blog/5-genius-ways-reuse-leftovers-tasty-meals) for more ideas.

## Banana Oat Pancakes

These pancakes use simple ingredients and no added sugar. Blend bananas with oats, eggs, and a little baking powder.

**Pancake Ingredients:**

- Ripe bananas
- Rolled oats
- Eggs
- Baking powder
- Cinnamon (optional)

Cook the batter in a pan until golden. The pancakes taste sweet and fluffy. They also make a healthy breakfast option. Natural sweetness from bananas eliminates the need for added sugar.

## Garlic Shrimp Skillet

Shrimp cooks fast and always delivers strong flavor. Heat olive oil in a pan and add garlic. Stir in the shrimp and cook until pink.

**Shrimp Cooking Tips:**

- Use fresh or frozen shrimp
- Don't overcook
- Add lemon at the end
- Serve immediately

Add lemon juice and salt. Serve with rice or pasta. The dish feels light yet satisfying. This recipe takes less than 15 minutes to prepare.

## Caprese Salad

This classic salad works well as a meal or side. Slice tomatoes and fresh mozzarella. Add basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil.

**Salad Components:**

- Fresh tomatoes
- Mozzarella cheese
- Fresh basil
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Sprinkle salt and pepper. The flavors taste fresh and clean. It is a perfect dish for warm days. This salad requires no cooking and comes together in minutes.

**Essential Staples:**

These elements boost flavor without adding complexity. Focus on quality rather than quantity.
