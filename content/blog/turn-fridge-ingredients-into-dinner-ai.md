## How to Scan Your Ingredients for Quick Results

![AI scanning fridge ingredients for dinner recipes](/images/blog/ai-scan-fridge-ingredients-dinner.svg)

Modern AI tools can scan your fridge with your phone camera. This step is quick and gives accurate results. Here's how it works:

1. Open the app on your smartphone
2. Point the camera at your ingredients
3. Let the tool detect everything automatically
4. Review and confirm the detected items

The system recognizes fruits, vegetables, sauces, and packaged foods. The process works even if items are unevenly placed. Once the scan finishes, the system creates a list that you can confirm or edit. Try our [ingredient scanning tool](/scan) to experience this yourself.

## Reduce Food Waste with Smarter Decisions

Food waste happens when ingredients expire before you use them. AI helps prevent this by suggesting meals that use items close to expiration. This means you cook what matters first.

**Waste Reduction Strategies:**

- Prioritize expiring ingredients
- Suggest recipes using older items first
- Track what you've already used
- Balance your cooking efficiently

It reduces waste and saves money over time. The system can also remind you when you already used similar ingredients in past meals. This keeps your cooking balanced and efficient.
